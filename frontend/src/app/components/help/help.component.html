Log - In Page

Email : Type-in the e-mail address, defined during the registration process.
Password : Type-in your personal password
Login: Press the button to enter the web banking application
 Bank-Transfer

Choose source Account: Select your bank account (IBAN), from the available drop-down list.
To Account: Select the IBAN account (either Internal or External) to which you wish to transfer (credit) your money.
Amount: Type the amount (in account currency) to transfer. Note that auto validations exists in case there is no available balance.
Note to Recipient: Free text to type a message / note to recipient.
Make Transfer: Press the button to complete the transfer
